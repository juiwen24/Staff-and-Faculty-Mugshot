Staff & Faculty Mugshot

This is a design of faculty mugshot pages created by Dora and me. We used 4 main colors in the design: yellow, orange, red, pink, purple, blue, green, and sage green. The order of top colors from the first page to the last page is: yellow, orange, red, pink, purple, blue, green, and sage green. The order of the bottom colors of the first page to the last page is the opposite of the top colors order(from sage green to yellow). The strips on the top and bottom of each page are created with a thin white rectangle and painted with colors using a brush effect brush. The white background makes the design on top stand out and decreases the complexity of the design since there are already many colors utilized. The frame of the image is created by two rectangles that are the same size(rectangle tool), the space where both rectangles overlap is where the image is placed. The background color of the frame is a gradient from top to bottom. We chose to use multiple colors because we wanted it to be colorful and playful we think faculty pages do not have to be serious because they are adults, it can be more interesting by using many colors to increase its childlikeness.

Through the process of making this design, I learned how to add a gradient onto unconnected rectangles.
